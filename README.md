# TouchGrassiOS
A social fitness iOS app where users compete with friends using real-time step tracking, daily and lifetime leaderboards, streaks, and live location updates.

--* Features *--
User Accounts: Create an account to start tracking steps and connect with friends by searching their unique usernames.
Friends & Social Network: Add friends and see your friends step totals via an individual Step Score that everyone you are connected with can view.
Step Score Counter: Tracks your individual total steps from the day you download the app with real-time updates.
Real-Time Friend Tracking: See the exact location and movement of your friends throughout the day with live updates.

--* Leaderboards *--
Daily Leaderboard: Shows how you performed compared to your social networks daily step score.
Overall Leaderboard: Shows entire socail networks total step score overall from the day they downloaded the app.
Global Leaderboard: Shows the top 100 users and their total step score overall as well as what position you are ranked globally (Ex. You are in 327th place).

--* Winning Streaks *--
- Daily Streak: Counts consecutive days you are first on the daily leaderboard against your friends.
- Overall Streak: Counts consecutive days you are first on the overall leaderboard against your friends. 

--* How It Works *--
-> Users sign up and add friends
-> The app reads step data (using HealthKit or device pedometer) to continuously calculate the Step Score
-> Daily and overall leaderboards are automatically updated in real-time
-> Winning streaks are tracked separately for daily and overall rankings against your network
-> Users can view live location and movement of friends in their network

--* Usage *--
-> Sign up or log in
-> Add friends by searching their usernames
-> Track your Step Score and monitor real-time friend leaderboards
-> Check your daily and overall win streaks against your friends
-> Compete against your friends in friendly step competition everyday
-> Use the map view to see your friends’ live movements
